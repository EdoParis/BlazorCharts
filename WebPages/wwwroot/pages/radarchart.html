Radarchart 1.2.0

A radar chart, also known as a spider chart, is a type of chart used to visualize multivariate data. The chart consists of a series of spokes radiating out from a central point, with each spoke representing a different variable. The data is plotted on each spoke using a line or area, and the end result looks like a spider web or star.
